  • What is JCC

    Joint Cloud Computing (JCC) is a recent key project funded by China’s Ministry of Science and Technology as a part of the National Key Program for Cloud Computing and Big Data.

  • JCC Basic Idea

    The concept of JCC is inspired by ideas from airline alliances and aims at empowering the cooperation among various cloud vendors to provide cross-cloud services via software definition.

    ranging from top Chinese universitie to major cloud vendors such as AliCloud, UCloud,JinShanCloud .
